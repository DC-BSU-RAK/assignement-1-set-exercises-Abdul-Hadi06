Creamy Chicken Lasagna

The Creamy Chicken Lasagna stands out as a cheesy masterpiece that brings deliciousness to any dining occasion. The lasagna brings together juicy tender chicken, rich creamy béchamel sauce, tomato sauce and a cheesy topping that melts just right in every bite. The combination of flavors and creamy texture makes it a perfect dish to enjoy with family or on special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna sheets (pre-cooked or oven-ready)
  • 2 cups of cooked, shredded chicken
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 2 cups of tomato puree
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 cups of warm milk
  • ½ cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to add taste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 2 cups of white sauce
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup of grated cheddar cheese
  • Fresh parsley or basil for garnish (optional)

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, then cook the chopped onion and minced garlic until it becomes soft, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add the shredded chicken and mushrooms, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Add the tomato puree, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Mix well, then remove from the heat and set it aside.
  5. In another pan melt the butter then add the flour and whisk for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Slowly add the warm milk while stirring until the sauce thickens, about 5 minutes.
  7. Mix in the parmesan cheese and add salt and pepper to add taste.
  8. In a baking dish, start with a thin layer of white sauce.
  9. Layer with lasagna sheets then add the chicken mixture and more white sauce.
  10. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, ending with white sauce on top.
  11. Finish with a top layer of white sauce and sprinkle the mozzarella and cheddar cheese on top.
  12. Cover with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes, then take off the foil and bake for another 10 minutes until the cheese turns golden.
  13. Wait a few minutes before serving the lasagna and add some fresh parsley or basil on top if you want.
